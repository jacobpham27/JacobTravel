Welcome to my page!

My name is Jacob Pham and it has always been my dream to travel the world and absorb multiple different cultures. I think it's very important to be open minded and understand where other people come from. Getting to know your neighbors is the first step towards peace. These are all of the places I want to visit in the future.

Vietnam

I'm Vietnamese, so of course this place had to be at the top of my list. As an Asian American who has never set foot in the motherland, I'm curious to see how different people live over there. I plan on learning the language to maximize my experience in absorbing my ancestors culture.

Netherlands

I've read numerous novels that really glorified the Netherlands which is what peaked my interest in the Netherlands. After doing a little bit of research and viewing many pictures, I do believe that this is one of the most beautiful places to visit.

Korea

I have always been a fan of Korean pop culture and food. Watching lifestyle videos from Korean people make it seem like they're years ahead of us. With such beautfiul sites to see and good food to eat, a trip to Korea has always been one of my dream destinations.
